1. Fill out all sections of this template completely.
2. If `Evidence` requires external documents, provide direct links.
3. Submit the completed proposal to the `governance/proposed-changes.log`.
4. The Architectural Review Board will evaluate the proposal and move it to either `approved-changes.log` or `rejected-changes.log`.
